These risk calculators provide an estimate of the risk of morbidity and mortality within 30 days of emergency abdominal surgery utilising published repository data. The NELA risk prediction tool has been shown to be more accurate than the P-POSSUM calculator for patients undergoing emergency laparotomy.

Theses tools should not be used in isolation but as part of the overall clinical decision making process to aid multidisciplinary planning and discussions with the patient and their family.

The author is not responsible for decisions made by health care professionals and patients which are made based on the estimations provided by herein.

The core algorithm of the NELA risk prediction calculator has CE marking from the Medicines & Healthcare Products Regulatory Agency (MHRA).
